Classic Waffles

Home

Description

These waffles turn out lovely and crispy. Perfect for any day of the week!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Step

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step

    3. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.

  3. Step

    4. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.

  4. Step

    5. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.

  5. Step

    6. Ladle batter into a preheated waffle iron.

  6. Step

    7. Cook waffles until golden and crisp.

  7. Step

    8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
